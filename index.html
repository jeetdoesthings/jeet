ABOUT

Jeet is a photographer and videographer based in India. His work uses simple light, clear compositions, and a natural sense of timing to bring out the small details in everyday moments. This gives his photos and videos a subtle, cinematic feel rooted in observation and mood. Through Jeet's lens, everyday life appears honest, calm, and thoughtfully captured.
